# Hardware documentation 

## List of components
Here is a list of each component with a link and some additional notes:
- **WiFi R3 ATmega328P (NodeMCU Compatible for Arduino Uno)** [arduino]([https://roboticx.ps/product/arduino-mega-2560-r3-china/](https://roboticx.ps/product/wifi-r3-atmega328p-nodemcu-compatible-for-arduino-uno/))
  - Operating voltage 7–12V, 14 Digital I/O Pins (6 PWM outputs) – 6 Analog Inputs, dimensions: 68.6 x 53.4 mm
  - Combines an ATmega328P microcontroller with WiFi connectivity, allowing wireless communication and IoT-based monitoring and control in Arduino Uno–compatible projects.
- **Battery**
  - Bundled with car, 4.2V, 5000mAh,Lithium, [Lithium Ion Battery](https://roboticx.ps/product/lithium-ion-battery-18650-cell-5000mah/)
- **Lithium Battery Charger Module**
  - Maximum current can be 5A , Output voltage 5V
- **Transistor — NPN (BC547)** [rransistor]([https://roboticx.ps/product/rocker-switch-on-off-spst/](https://roboticx.ps/product/transistor-npn-bc547/))
    - General-purpose NPN transistor, commonly used for switching and amplification in low-power electronic circuits.
- **Piezo Element** [Piezo Element](https://roboticx.ps/product/piezo-element/?asp_highlight=piezoe&p_asid=3)
  - Converts mechanical energy (pressure or vibration) into electrical energy, enabling energy harvesting from movement.
- **Capacitor** [Capacitor](https://roboticx.ps/product/capacitor/)
  - Used to store electrical energy generated by the piezoelectric sensor and supply it to the LED for short-duration illumination.
- **Diode Rectifier** [diode]([https://roboticx.ps/product/capacitor/](https://roboticx.ps/product/diode-rectifier-1a-50v/))
  -Allows current to flow in one direction only, used to convert AC signals from the piezoelectric sensor into DC for charging the capacitor and powering the LED.Used to store electrical energy generated by the     piezoelectric sensor and supply it to the LED for short-duration illumination.
- **Resistors** [Resistors]([https://roboticx.ps/product/capacitor/](https://roboticx.ps/product/diode-rectifier-1a-50v/))
  - 4* Resistors (1M)ohm (10,100)k ohm
  - Used to control current flow, divide voltage, and protect components within the circuit
  **I2C 16x02 Serial LCD for Arduino & RPI** [I2C]([https://roboticx.ps/product/capacitor/](https://roboticx.ps/product/diode-rectifier-1a-50v/)](https://roboticx.ps/product/i2c-16x02-serial-lcd-for-arduino-rpi/))
  - Displays text and basic characters from the microcontroller with minimal pin usage, ideal for Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects.


## Sensors communication protocols
The different communication protocols used:

   - Piezo Element `Analog`
    - Ardunio `USB `
      - LED `Digital (ON/OFF)`
      - Transistor (BC547) `Digital switching`
      - 2C 16x02 Serial LCD `I2C`
